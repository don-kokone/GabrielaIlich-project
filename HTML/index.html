aBOUT Me

Born and raised in Germany, Gabriela Ilich developed a love for modeling at the age of 16. She is from the city Munich in Bavaria. Her fierce and natural looks and fun personality can be seen through her images.

As a model, Gabriela has been on the runway several times and worked for Shape Magazine, Leonisa, Women'secret, Stell Magazine, Zalando Deutschland and more.
